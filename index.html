Jamhuriya University of Science and Technology (JUST):

waa mid ka mid ah jaamacadaha hormuudka ka ah Soomaaliya, laguna yaqaan bixinta waxbarasho tayo sare leh. Jaamacaddu waxaa la aasaasay sanadkii 2011, iyadoo hadafkeedu yahay inay soo saarto arday aqoon, xirfad, iyo anshax wanaagsan leh. JUST waxay bixisaa barnaamijyo kala duwan oo dhinacyada Teknolojiyadda, Caafimaadka, Ganacsiga, Injineernimada, iyo Waxbarashada ah, iyadoo ardayda u diyaarinaysa inay noqdaan hoggaamiyeyaasha mustaqbalka. Hal-ku-dhigga jaamacaddu waa "Home of Quality Education".
